# Microsoft Copilot for Product Managers - Course Index

## Course Overview

**Title:** Microsoft Copilot for Product Managers
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Duration:** 6 hours
**Target Audience:** Product Managers

A comprehensive course teaching Product Managers to leverage Microsoft Copilot for everyday work, from basic tasks to advanced strategic workflows.

---

## Course Structure

### Level 1: Foundation (~3.5 hours)
Core Copilot skills for PMs - 10 modules covering the fundamentals.

| Module | Title | Duration | Key Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|------------|
| 1.1 | Welcome to Microsoft Copilot | 15 min | What Copilot is, accessing it, meet TaskFlow |
| 1.2 | Setting Up Your Workspace | 15 min | Optimal setup, file uploads, ContextIQ, OneDrive |
| 1.3 | Your First PM Tasks | 30 min | Meeting notes, interview synthesis, communications |
| 1.4 | Researcher & Analyst Agents | 25 min | Deep research, data reasoning, workflow combinations |
| 1.5 | Persona Prompting | 20 min | Effective persona prompts, multi-perspective feedback |
| 1.6 | Building Context with Memory | 15 min | Copilot Memory, context documents, persistence |
| 1.7 | Copilot Across M365 Apps | 15 min | Word, Excel, PowerPoint, keyboard shortcuts |
| 1.8 | Copilot in Microsoft Teams | 25 min | Meeting summaries, action items, chats, channels |
| 1.9 | Copilot in Outlook | 20 min | Email drafting, tone adjustment, thread summaries |
| 1.10 | Copilot in SharePoint | 20 min | Document search, site summaries, collaboration |

### Level 2: Advanced PM Workflows (~1.5 hours)
Complex PM tasks with Copilot - 3 modules for experienced users.

| Module | Title | Duration | Key Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|------------|
| 2.1 | Writing PRDs with Copilot | 35 min | Thinking partner, PRD sections, multi-perspective feedback |
| 2.2 | Analyzing Product Data | 30 min | Data uploads, Excel Copilot, A/B test interpretation |
| 2.3 | Product Strategy | 30 min | Strategic frameworks, competitive analysis, roadmap prioritization |

### Level 3: Product Visuals (~45 minutes)
Creating visuals with DALL-E and Designer - 2 modules on image generation.

| Module | Title | Duration | Key Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|------------|
| 3.1 | Image Generation Basics | 20 min | DALL-E 3, effective prompts, iteration |
| 3.2 | PM Visuals | 25 min | Persona illustrations, journey maps, marketing assets |
